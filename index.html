About Me

I’m a 25 year old student at UCSD, majroing in Cognitive Science and minoring in Computer Science. My passion for both the humanities and sciences probably stemmed from my upbringing - raised by a preschool teacher and an engineer, I quickly developed a curiosity and appreciation for both people and ideas. This translated to a wide variety of hobbies and interests - from salsa dancing and jewelry making to neuroscience and web programming.

What this range of experiences has taught me is that there is always another way to see things - there is always room to learn and grow, and to see things from another perspective. I also realized that it’s impossible to know everything, and so I strive to stay flexible and open to criticism.

Although my educational path has led me to a highly technical field, I recognize that the tools and technologies I will be a part of creating exist in a social and political context. The human element is very important to me and I hope to bring that perspective to whatever initiative I am a part of.
